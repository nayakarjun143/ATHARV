Nitish Kumar Reddy

Kaki Nitish Kumar Reddy is an Indian cricketer. He plays for Andhra in domestic cricket and Sunrisers Hyderabad in the Indian Premier League. He made his first-class debut on 27 January 2020, for Andhra in the 2019–20 Ranji Trophy.

Early Life and Career

Nitish Kumar Reddy was born on 26 May 2003 in Vishakhapatnam, Andhra Pradesh, and is the son of former Hindustan Zinc employee Mutyala Reddy
